# Androidacy Code of Conduct

## Introduction

At Androidacy, we are committed to fostering an open, inclusive, supportive, and respectful community for everyone who participates in our repositories and communities. This Code of Conduct outlines our expectations for participant behavior and the consequences for unacceptable behavior.

This Code of Conduct applies to all Androidacy community spaces, including GitHub repositories, issue trackers, pull requests, discussions, and any other communication channel operated by or on behalf of Androidacy, LLC, both online and offline.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community. We value and respect all individuals irrespective of their race, religion, sexual orientation, gender identity, age, nationality, disability, appearance, or technical expertise.

## Our Standards

### Expected Behavior

We expect all participants in Androidacy communities to:

- Demonstrate empathy and kindness towards other people.
- Respect personal identities and experiences, particularly related to LGBTQ+, race, religion, sexuality, gender, and disability.
- Give and gracefully accept constructive feedback.
- Take responsibility for mistakes, apologize to those affected, and learn from the experience.
- Focus on what is best not just for us as individuals, but for the overall community.

### Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are prohibited:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any form, whether public or private.
- The use of sexualized language or imagery, and sexual attention or advances of any kind.
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks.
- Publishing others' private information (such as physical addresses, email addresses, phone numbers, financial information, or real names associated with pseudonymous accounts) without their explicit permission.
- Sustained disruption of community discussions or events.
- Any conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Reporting and Enforcement

If you are the subject of or witness any violation of this Code of Conduct, please report it by emailing community@androidacy.com.

Androidacy will review all reports promptly and will maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter of an incident to the extent practicable. Androidacy reserves the right, in its sole discretion, to take any action it deems appropriate in response to a violation, including but not limited to:

- A private warning to the individual.
- Temporary or permanent removal of content.
- Temporary suspension from community participation.
- Permanent ban from all Androidacy community spaces.

Androidacy is not obligated to act on any report or to provide a specific explanation for enforcement decisions. Enforcement decisions are final at Androidacy's discretion and are not subject to appeal under this Code of Conduct. Nothing in this Code of Conduct creates a right of appeal or review; however, to the extent an enforcement action gives rise to a dispute under the Androidacy Terms of Service, such dispute is subject to the dispute resolution provisions of the ToS at androidacy.com/terms/.
